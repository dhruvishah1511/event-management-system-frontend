Business & professional events

Networking events

Networking events are like social mixers for your career, letting you meet cool folks and discover fresh opportunities. For example, Speak Up Entertainment’s Goal-Getters Business Networking Dinner invites business owners from all industries to come together, share a meal, and bounce ideas back and forth with like-minded individuals.

Training seminars

Training seminars are invaluable sessions for growth, boosting both professional and business skills. Through seminars like JumpStart Inc’s Small Business Training Camp attendees learn the fundamentals of small business capital. Events like this empower event-goers to confidently navigate the business world.

Workshops

In focused environments, like a targeted workshop, attendees sharpen specific skills and connect with peers. At Carla Stiles’ LinkedIn B2B Workshop, participants learn to harness LinkedIn for business growth and online visibility, all while networking with fellow business owners.

Music events

Festivals

Music festivals aren’t just about the music. They’re culture events where beats echo and spirits soar — a shared joy for all who attended.

Instrument clinics

Instrument clinics are like mini masterclasses, helping attendees level up their skills and discover new approaches to their instruments.

Local performances

Music festivals and concerts aren’t the only way to share the joy of music. Smaller, more intimate events, like Muggswigz Coffee & Tea’s Acoustic Open Mic Nights, offer a cozy setting where local artists can connect with audiences on a personal level.

Food & drink events

Food truck festivals

Food truck festivals are a big hit, serving a smorgasbord of diverse cuisines for all food lovers to enjoy. Need inspiration? Look at the Portsmouth Food Truck & Craft Beer Festival hosted by Food Truck Festivals of America.

Cooking classes

Cooking classes are always a fun, explorative experience. Attendees get to sharpen their kitchen game, explore delicious recipes, and walk away with some tasty dishes they whipped up themselves.

Cooking competitions

Who doesn’t love a cooking competition? They’re a perfect way to showcase local culinary talents and stir up some friendly rivalry, all while serving unforgettable dishes.

Performance & visual arts events

Art expos

Imagine stepping into an art space where tradition meets modern flair, where the boundaries of conventional art expos are playfully pushed.

Galas & dinners

Galas are elegant and exciting entertainment events that bring people together for a memorable evening. But not all galas have to be the “Met Ball.” Get creative.

Retreats

Imagine pausing the daily grind of work and life for a few days and attending an event where everyone shares a similar passion. This is where the magic of retreats comes into play.
